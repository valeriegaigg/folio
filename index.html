Fittrack - Agile Project

Fittrack is a fictitious business website for users to begin their personalized fitness journey. As a self-guided project for my Web Development Capstone, I was placed on an Agile team with 3 other student developers. The team collaborated through many stages of project planning, development, and testing during a 17 week period.

I focused mainly on front-end development for this project, while occasionally supporting where needed. The biggest takeaway from this experience was learning how to communicate and develop as a team, while keeping our project deadlines in mind.

Jennifer Tabbert Photography - Website Renovation

This project is a website redesign for a local small business photographer. After realizing how out of date the current website was, I decided to propose a new website to the business owner. After discussing places to improve this website, I drafted and presented the first set of wireframes. From here, I spent time making adjustments where needed, analyzing opportunities for SEO, and workly closely with the business owner to create an updated website. I used the Next.js framework, along with React components, to ensure overall website optimization.

This site is set to launch May 2026.

DVD Central Project

DVD Central is a e-commerce website where customers can browse for and purchase movies. During my ASP.NET class, I created this website as part of the coursework. While it lacks aesthetics, I was able to learn the in's and out's of Model-View-Controller framework. I gained confidence in my back-end programming skills throughout the course of this 17 week project. I was also introduced to Azure Devops, where I learned and practiced using project management software.
