Fittrack Project

Fittrack is a fictitious business website for users to begin their personalized fitness journey. As a self-guided project for my Web Development Capstone, I was placed on an Agile team with 3 other student developers. The team collaborated through many stages of project planning, development, and testing to bring this ASP.NET project to life over a 17 week period.

I focused mainly on front-end development for this project, while occasionally supporting where needed. The biggest takeaway from this experience was learning how to communicate and develop as a team, while keeping our project deadlines in mind.
